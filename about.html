About

Our bakers start work before the sun rises each day to ensure that every batch is as fresh as possible before it gets to you. The warm smell of baking often escapes onto the street before we've even opened. We're dedicated to kickstarting your morning with warm cinnamon rolls, buttery biscuits and a cup of hot coffee!

We work with local suppliers to source the freshest and most authentic ingredients, and use old-fashioned techniques to ensure the highest quality. Our high quality meats are smoked in house, and are sure to make your tastebuds happy.
